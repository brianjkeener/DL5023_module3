About This Resource

As educators in the 21st century, developing Technological Pedagogical Knowledge (TPK) is essential. Drawing on the ISTE Standards and Project-Based Learning (PBL) principles, this collection helps teachers navigate the digital landscape to enhance student creativity and design digital-age assessments.
