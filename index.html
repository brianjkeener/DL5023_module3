About This Resource

As educators in the 21st century, developing Technological Pedagogical Knowledge (TPK) is essential for effective instruction. Drawing on the principles of the ISTE Standards and Project-Based Learning (PBL), this collection aims to support teachers in navigating the digital landscape. These resources were selected to help you enhance student creativity, design digital-age assessments, and foster a future-ready classroom environment.
